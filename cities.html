Cities I Lived In

Before coming to the USA, I've lived in some of the most happening cities in India. Below are some of the cities I've lived for both work and personal use.

  • Hyderabad, India

    Almost half of my life, I have lived in Hyderabad. I worked there for the last 5 years and had some of my best memories of my life with my friends and loved ones. It is one of the most happening places with respect to events and people.

    Rating: ★★★★★

  • Warangal, Telangana

    Being my hometown, I have very special memories of my childhood. I did my schooling and spent around 8 years in Warangal. Being a small town, I know almost all the local restaurants and the food there is amazing. If i get a chance, I would like to stay there when I'm old and retire.

    Rating: ★★★★★

  • Coorg, India

    The trip to Coorg was absolutely refreshing which make me feel alive, so, I stayed there for almost 1 month. The journey through winding roads, surrounded by lush green hills, was the perfect escape from the city. I worked from there remotely and had a great time. One of the highlights was visiting Abbey Falls which was worth the trip and visited it like 5 times in the same month.

    Rating: ★★★★☆
